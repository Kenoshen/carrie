As a kid, I always loved putting together magic shows and starting new lemonade selling companies. I would design flyers and posters to put up all over my neighborhood. I continued to explore my creative talents by taking my first graphic design class in high school and absolutely fell in love with web and graphic design.

Continuing my education with a Bachelor of Science Degree, I am currently majoring in Web Design and Interactive Media with an emphasis in Graphic Design at Harding University. I thrive under the pressure of deadlines and continuously challenge myself with new goals and creating smarter designs.

If I could describe myself in three words they would be, passionate (because I firmly believe if you love what you do you never work a day in your life), dedicated (because it is my goal to seek perfection in what I create) and creative (because I enjoy the challenge of bringing new ideas to life).
